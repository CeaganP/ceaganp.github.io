About Me

For the past 4 years I have been learning computers inside and out. From the way they're built, to how they run, why they fail and what you want from them.

At my previous employment I spent a large portion of my time with application development and upkeep. The experience gave me a wealth of knowledge in testing, UI design, functionality and performance. It isn't just about how long your program takes to run, but the impact on the systems other processes.

I have spent a large portion of time working on personal projects. I taught myself how to integrate two languages together to appear as one application. Using the C++ Framework in a C# application to modify the brightness of a monitor. An application is currently in development which will allow users to create an easily distributable set of hotkeys.
