About Me

For the past 4 years I have been learning computers inside and out. From the way they're built, to how they run, why they fail and what you want from them.
Recently a more in depth experience in developing applications in C# and C++ has been gained. At Timberland Equipment I spent a large portion of my time doing custom application development for internal use. The experience gave me a wealth of knowledge in testing, UI design, functionality and performance. It isn't just about how long your program takes to run, but the impact on the systems other processes.
In addition my experience at Timberland I have spent a large portion of time working on personal projects. I taught myself how to integrate two languages together to appear as one application. Using the C++ Framework in a C# application to modify the brightness of a monitor.
